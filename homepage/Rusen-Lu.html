Research
The research interests include high-resolution radio astrophysics, in particular Very Long Baseline Interferometry (VLBI) and its applications in astrophysics.
It has long been interested in the imaging of supermassive black holes and has carried out a series of high-resolution studies on the central black holes of the Milky Way SgrA*, M87 and other nearby active galactic nuclei. These results have been published in top international academic journals, including Science. Among them, the structure of galactic black holes at the event horizon scale is selected as one of the most commonly used AAS 2018. (over 20,000 times).
